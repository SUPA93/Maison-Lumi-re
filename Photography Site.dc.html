About the Studio

Twelve years of witnessing love, unscripted.

Marlowe & Co. was founded on a simple idea: the best wedding photography disappears into the day itself. We move quietly, shoot instinctively, and hand back a story that feels exactly like it did to live it.

From intimate elopements in Sonoma to five-day celebrations in Tuscany, our approach stays the same — genuine light, honest emotion, and an archive you'll return to for decades.
